Resume

I am a graduate composer and classical guitarist from Rome, Italy. I have studied, composed and played with Maestro Eugenio Becherucci and Maestro Luca Salvadori. My artistic production focuses on finding a merging point between tradition and innovation, hoping to communicate with the audience as candidly as possible.

  • Full artistic resume (IT)

Education

  • 2013 Bachelor Degree in D.A.M.S. (Discipline of Music, Art and Show, 110 cum laude) at the Tor Vergata University of Rome
  • 2015 Guitar Diploma (10/10) at Conservatory Licinio Refice of Frosinone
  • 2023 Master Degree in Composition (110 cum laude), in the same Conservatory
  • 2023 Contemporary Composition for Baroque Instruments’ masterclass at the Accademia Chigiana in Siena
  • 2022/2023 Opera Composition’s course at Accademia dei Mestieri dell’Opera del Teatro Coccia in Novara
